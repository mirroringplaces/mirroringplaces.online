### Welcome to
## Mirroring Places

<img src="images/shamanObj.png" width="100%" class="stickyMap" id="imgShaman">
<div class="textBox">
<p>Mirroring Places, an archeology of memories / Arqueologias da Memória, o espaço e seus duplos is a hypermedia choreography that takes place in two locations, in the Bixiga - Bela Vista neighborhood in São Paulo and in Lundtoftegade in Copenhagen. Created from interviews with inhabitants of these two territories, this exhibition is made up of sculptures and panels physically installed in the locations and a digital archive that links memories, objects and spaces.</p></div><br>
<div class="textBox">   
<p>Choose which territory's archive you want to access by clicking on the silhouettes below; then browse through the scenes and chapters indicated on the maps and in the captions.</p><br>
<p>To access the exhibition as an augmented reality experience, visit the locations and find the access codes on the sculptures and panels installed. For more information on where and when to find these installations, continue browsing.
</p>
</div>
